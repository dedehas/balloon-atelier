My story

When the balloon lady working for @mrstipi_marbella let her down, she called me to help her out with the party she was setting up. At first, I thought that all I'd need to do was just blow up a few balloons. When I realised what Tatyana really needed, I nearly fainted! I didn't give it a second thought and enrolled on a course straightaway, determined not to let my friend's reputation down.

On the big day, I made plenty of rookie mistakes - picture balloons flying out of my car windows! But guess what? The display turned out beautifully, and I have not looked back since. I've been attending workshops and seminars to stay on top of the latest trends. I'm always working to hone my skills so I can create curated displays for any celebration, to help transform your space into something magical.
